The UCC OpenID service lets you use you UCC username as an OpenID of the form http://username.ucc.asn.au/. You can use it after a simple addition to your UCC homepage:

Add the following lines to the index page in your ~/public-html directory:

<link rel="openid.server" href="https://secure.ucc.asn.au/openid/" />

That's it! You can now enter http://username.ucc.asn.au/ as your OpenID on compatible websites and login using your UCC username and password.

Coming soon: Delegated OpenID authentication to Tartarus (the University of Western Australia central student server).

For more information see the OpenID website.

UCC OpenID is written in Python using Web.py, including flup session middleware with extensions by Adam Atlas (further modified), and the open-source JanRain OpenID library.
